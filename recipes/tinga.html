Mexican Tinga

Description

This tinga recipe, which features shredded chicken in a spicuy and smoky tonmato-adobo, is a great mexican dinner to feed a crowd. These are the ingredients you will need to make this homemade chicken tonga recipe :

  • Onion and oil: This chicken tinga recipe starts with a large dices onion cooked in olive oil.
  • Canned goods: you will need a can of stewed tomatoes and a can of chipotle peppers in adobo sauce.
  • Chicken: Cook and shred your own chicken or use leftover rotisserie chicken for this tinga recipe.
  • Tostada shells: serve the flavorful chicken tinga over tosdtada shells
  • Sour cream: Finish each servin with a dollop of sour cream.

,

How to make Chicken Tinga

Here is a brief overview of what you can expect when you make chicken tinga :

  1. Cook the onion.
  2. Puree the tomatoes and chipotle peppers in a blender.
  3. Add the puree to the onions on the stove, stir in the chicken, and simmer.
  4. Dollop the chicken mixture onto tostadas and top with sour cream.
